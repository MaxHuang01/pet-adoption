My Story

This charming 3-year-old gal is looking for a companion to spend furever with! Nala is an incredibly sweet girl who was recently returned after spending 2 years in a home. At the shelter she is very stressed out by the excessive noise. In her kennel, Nala looks SOOO sad you would never guess she can be SUPER fun and playful when she opens up… and it doesn’t take too long! If you respect her process, she’s happy to make new friends, especially when they’re down to play some fetch with her squeaky toys! She was spoiled in her last home and loved sitting in her person’s lap and sleeping with them in their bed. Nala was mainly returned because her family was moving, but she did struggle with a few issues like chewing and digging when left unsupervised. These issues were likely due to separation anxiety and latent puppy behavior, for which our Behavior department can provide lifelong support.
